What Did We Build?

At the minimum, the ecosystem would include:
    - mobile app for patients
    - admin area for RTHM
The mobile app would allow patients to monitor their conditions, track symptoms, and follow treatment plans, while physicians would diagnose and recommend treatment based on patients' PHI in the dashboard.

-----Getting M in MVP right-----

The worst thing you can do with a project of this scale is to rush it, trying to do everything at once.
Fortunately, the founders recognized the iterative nature of Agile and embraced the no-code/low-code approach.
New research continues to emerge on long COVID on a daily basis, so everyone had to be comfortable pivoting frequently.

To help RTHM create a minimum viable product, we focused on:
    - Researching tech feasibility of ready-made solutions, such as Apple's       ResearchKit and CareKit, or ResearchStack for Android
    - Exploring compatibility of multiple EHRs, frameworks and 3rd party services
    - Rapid prototyping of patient and provider user experiences

As a result, we supported the design of a scalable and secure architecture that would help the ecosystem mature without running into typical growth hiccups. The best part? With the no-code tools, we managed to ship the MVP much faster.

-----Taking Care of V in MVP-----

To make the product viable (read valuable) for customers, our team zeroed in on its engagement capacity:
    - Intuitive and welcoming design
    - Interactive charts
    - Automated reminders via SMS
    - Tips and instructions for patients
    - Digitizing medical treatment protocols

This helped establish the software as a trusted ally for patients and physicians in their daily lives, leading to improved collection of actionable health data.

-----Wearables Are More Than Trend-----

Accurately diagnosing and treating ME/CFS and Long COVID implies meticulous analysis of a myriad of health data points.
To make this PHI available to physicians and for automated data analysis, we explored integrating with 3rd party services which in turn normalized health data across multiple health and fitness ecosystems such as Apple Health and Google Fit.
All data travels within the ecosystem, following strict HIPAA compliance guidelines.

-----It Takes Two to Tango-----
While RTHM worked on a no-code back-end platform for physicians, Topflight integrated it with a custom-built mobile patient application. Data models and flows were in continual transition, making our agile development approach crucial for the two teams to interact seamlessly.
The ecosystem was built out one step at a time by successfully coordinating both teams’ complementary skill sets into a great joint delivery.

Trusted Platform for healthcare workers

Mar 2018 - Oct 2023


Overview

Medely is a platform designed to connect healthcare professionals with temporary and permanent job opportunities, was a meaningful and complex project.
The platform aimed to streamline the hiring process for healthcare facilities and professionals by providing a marketplace where qualified healthcare workers, like nurses and medical technicians, could find shifts and full-time positions.
My role as a full-stack engineer involved developing a scalable and user-friendly system that managed job listings, applications, scheduling, and payments, all while ensuring the platform adhered to strict security and compliance standards due to the sensitive nature of healthcare data.

Main Features

    - Real-Time Job Matching
    - Scheduling and Availability Management
    - Credential Verification and Compliance
    - In-App Messaging
    - Automated Payments
    - Job Search Filters and Alerts
    - User Ratings and Reviews

Challenges & Solutions

    - Ensuring Data Security and HIPAA Compliance
    - Real-Time Updates for Job Matching
    - Scaling the Platform for High User Demand
    - Handling Complex Scheduling and Availability

Conclustion

Building Medely was a highly rewarding experience that combined my expertise in full-stack development with the needs of the healthcare industry.

The platform’s ability to match healthcare professionals with job opportunities in real-time, streamline scheduling, and ensure secure financial transactions helped solve major staffing challenges in healthcare.

With its focus on scalability, security, and user experience, Medely became a trusted platform for healthcare workers and facilities alike, making a significant impact in a fast-paced and critical industry.
